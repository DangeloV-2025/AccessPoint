About Access Point

At AccessPoint, we are dedicated to empowering and uplifting students by connecting them with resources and organizations needed to achieve their academic goals. We understand that navigating the path to higher education can be difficult. Filtering through scholarships, fly-in programs, application requirements, and financial aid packages can be overwhelming. That’s why we’re here – to make the process simpler, more accessible, and tailored to every student’s unique journey.

Our Mission

At Access Point, we are dedicated to uplifting and empowering underrepresented high school students. We connect students with opportunities like scholarships, pre-college programs, and fly-in programs to enhance their readiness and ability to succeed in undergraduate education.

Our mission is to simplify the process of navigating higher education by providing tailored resources for each student’s unique journey.
