Pre-College Programs

Pre-college programs give high school students a chance to dive into academic interests, get ready for college, and experience campus lifeâ€”usually during the summer. These programs can be anything from week-long workshops to full summer courses.

Students often live in dorms, take advanced classes, explore potential majors, or get hands-on experience in areas like engineering, writing, or science. Along the way, they pick up key skills for college, like time management, critical thinking, and problem-solving.

Participating in a pre-college program can also make college applications stronger by showing genuine interest in a subject and a commitment to learning. On top of that, these programs connect students with diverse peers, faculty, and resources, helping them figure out how they might fit into different universities.
